Our Journey & Story

Welcome to Bombay Chaupati Fast Food Corner, Agra's ultimate destination for authentic, mouth-watering street food and fast-food delicacies. Located right in front of Holi Public School, Bhawna Estate, Sikandra, we have been serving happiness on a plate to our local food community.

We specialize in crafting unique fusion dishes like our famous Pizza Momos, highly-demanded Tandoori Chaap, and perfectly wok-tossed Chinese Noodles. Our mission is simple: to bring the vibrant, rich, and tangy flavours of street-style fast food with absolute premium-grade kitchen hygiene.
